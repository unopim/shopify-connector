# Shopify Connector - Feature List

## Store Credentials
- Add and manage multiple Shopify store credentials from one place.
- Connect your Shopify store to UnoPim in one click using the UnoPIM-Shopify Connector App.
- Save separate credential profiles for each Shopify store and switch between them when running jobs.
- Sync products and data with multiple Shopify stores from a single UnoPim instance.

## Export Attribute Mapping
- Map your UnoPim product attributes to the matching Shopify fields so the right data goes to the right place during export.

## Import Attribute Mapping
- Map Shopify product fields to UnoPim attributes so imported data is stored correctly.

## Meta Fields
- Configure Shopify metafields (text, number, JSON, and more) for your products.
- Send custom product details from UnoPim to Shopify as metafields, and bring Shopify metafields back into UnoPim.

## Settings
- Map your UnoPim locales to Shopify locales for multi-language stores.
- Control additional export options such as tags and metafield behavior per store.

## Export from UnoPim to Shopify
- **Products** — export simple and variant products along with attributes, images, and related data.
- **Categories** — export your UnoPim categories as Shopify collections.
- **Metafields** — export your configured metafields to Shopify.
- **Multi-language export** — send product data to Shopify in multiple languages based on your locale mapping.
- **Filters** — limit what gets exported by channel, currency, or specific product SKUs.

## Import from Shopify to UnoPim
- **Products** — import Shopify products into UnoPim with your chosen channel, locale, and currency.
- **Collections** — import Shopify collections as UnoPim categories with locale mapping.
- **Attributes** — import Shopify product options and custom attributes into UnoPim.
- **Families** — import Shopify product types as attribute families and assign them to an attribute group.
- **Metafields** — import Shopify metafields into UnoPim with locale mapping.
- **Multi-language import** — bring in product data from Shopify in multiple languages.

## Multi-Regional Support
- **Multi-currency** — work with stores and products across different currencies.
- **Multi-language** — sync product content in multiple languages between UnoPim and Shopify.
